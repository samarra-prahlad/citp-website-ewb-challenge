A low-impact, community-governed visitor accommodation design for Yintjingga (Port Stewart), Lama Lama Country, Cape York Peninsula.

Location Port Stewart, Cape York, QLD
Community partner Yintjingga Aboriginal Corporation
Subject CITP · UTS Autumn 2026

We acknowledge the Lama Lama people as the Traditional Custodians of Yintjingga (Port Stewart) and the surrounding Country. We pay our respects to Elders past and present, and recognise that sovereignty was never ceded. This design has been developed in the spirit of collaboration and respect for Lama Lama cultural protocols and land management authority.

The problem

Port Stewart needs accommodation that works for Country

How can low-impact visitor accommodation be provided at Port Stewart in a way that is culturally appropriate, environmentally sensitive, and maintainable by Lama Lama Rangers without outside expertise?
